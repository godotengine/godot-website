Discord

A vibrant community for discussion, user support, showcases... and custom emoji!

Godot Contributors Chat

Chat for engine contributors and developers based on Rocket.Chat.

Reddit

A community for discussion and support in development with the game engine.

GitHub

Twitter

YouTube

Channel for official Godot videos.

Facebook

Large community for discussions around Godot.

Forum

Community forum for all Godot developers.

Steam Community

Discuss and share tips with other developers on Steam.

Mastodon

Connect with us in the Fediverse.

IRC

Old-fashioned but reliable chat platform. Significantly less active than Discord. #godotengine@libera.chat

Matrix

Libre decentralized chat with advanced features, IRC compatible.
