2019

  • 21 March: Godot GDC Meetup, San Francisco

Godot GDC Meetup @ GitHub HQ

Like last year, Godot developers are going to GDC in San Francisco, and we're using the opportunity to have a Godot GDC Meetup at the GitHub HQ.

GitHub is generously hosting us again at their headquarters, and it will be a great opportunity to meet community members and play Godot games.

See the Eventbrite page for details and registration.

  • Date: Thu, 21 March 2019, 6:30 pm to 9:30 pm
  • Location: GitHub HQ, 88 Colin P Kelly Junior Street, San Francisco, CA 94107
  • Entrance fee: Free
  • Registration: Mandatory. Sign up on Eventbrite.
